About GL Consulting

A Level 1 B-BBEE certified, Young-Black-Woman-Owned Engineering Company.

At **GL Consulting**, we're committed to delivering innovative solutions and exceptional service across a diverse range of sectors. Founded in 2023, our dynamic team brings fresh perspectives and robust expertise to every project.

We pride ourselves on offering comprehensive services including **industrial supply, waste management, professional cleaning, specialized engineering, meticulous maintenance, and robust construction solutions.**

Our core strength lies in providing expert **consulting services** and dedicated **engineering project management**. From the initial concept to the final handover, we work tirelessly to ensure cost-effective, high-quality outcomes that precisely align with our clients' strategic goals.
