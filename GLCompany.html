GL Consulting

Empowering Innovation and Excellence

Founded in 2023

Welcome to GL Consulting, a Young-Black-Woman-Owned Engineering company dedicated to delivering innovative solutions and exceptional service.

We're a Level 1 B-BBEE certified company, proud to offer a wide range of services, including industrial supply, waste management, cleaning, engineering, maintenance, and construction.

Our team of experts is passionate about providing consulting services and engineering project management that drives results. From inception to final project handover, we're committed to optimizing cost-effective solutions that meet our clients' unique needs.
